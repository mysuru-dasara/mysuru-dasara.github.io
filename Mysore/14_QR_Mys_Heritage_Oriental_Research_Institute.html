Oriental research Institute

The Oriental Research Institute was established in Mysore by His Highness Sri. ChamarajaWodeyarBhahadur, the then Maharaja of Mysore, in the year 1891 October. In 1916, the University of Mysore was established and the Institute became an integral part of the Mysore University.  

Aims and Objectives
The aim of this Institution is to collect, preserve, collate, edit and publish the rare valuble Sanskrit and Kannada palm leaf as well as Paper Manuscripts of our nation.
